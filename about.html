ABOUT

Apsara Fund Management was established in 2021 by Mr. Thorn Vandong and Ms. Srors Chhon and operates under the regulations of the Ministry of Commerce in the Kingdom of Cambodia.

We are a trust company dedicated to helping settlers and investors succeed in the Cambodian market. We strive to deliver sustainable financial management solutions for impact investments by providing trust services with standards, professionalism, and trust. With a focus on institutional investors, angel investors, private equity firms, and family offices, we offer various types of trusts to help our customers achieve their ESG goals and commercial returns.

What truly sets us apart is our commitment to integrity, doing no harm, building an empire, and accountability values.

VISION

Apsara envisiones​​ the professional and sustainable investments in financial and asset management.

MISSION

Our platform is committed to delivering sustainable financial management solutions for impactful investments that are trustworthy and reliable.

GOALS

  • To strengthen institutional government, environmental and social inclusion to SME and other forms of business.

  • To promote/pionia accessibility and availability of impact investments.
